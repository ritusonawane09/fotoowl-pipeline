Corporate style guide.
Use medium, steady pacing, typically 2-3 seconds per photo.
Favor clean cuts and subtle slide transitions. Avoid flashy effects.
Color treatment: neutral, professional tones with balanced contrast.
Captions should be concise, informative, in a clean sans-serif font, appearing with simple fades.
Prioritize clear, well-composed shots: group photos, handshakes, presentations, branded moments.
Pacing should feel polished and professional, never rushed or overly casual.
