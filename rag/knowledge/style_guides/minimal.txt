Minimal style guide.
Use slow to medium pacing, typically 2-4 seconds per photo.
Favor simple cuts or gentle fades only. No slides, zooms, or flashy transitions.
Color treatment: muted, clean tones with lots of negative space.
Captions should be rare or absent; when used, small, understated, and unobtrusive.
Prioritize simple, uncluttered compositions over busy or crowded shots.
Pacing should feel calm and restrained, letting the imagery speak for itself.
