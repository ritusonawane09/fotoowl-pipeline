Cinematic style guide.
Use slow pacing with long dwell times per scene, typically 3-5 seconds per photo.
Favor fade and slow zoom transitions over hard cuts.
Color treatment: warm, slightly desaturated tones with soft contrast.
Captions should be minimal, appearing sparingly, in a thin serif or elegant font, timed to linger.
Emotional, intimate framing. Close-ups and quiet in-between moments are prioritized over group shots.
Music and pacing should feel unhurried, letting each moment breathe.
