Upbeat style guide.
Use fast pacing with short dwell times per scene, typically 1-2 seconds per photo.
Favor hard cuts and quick slide transitions over slow fades.
Color treatment: vibrant, high-saturation tones with punchy contrast.
Captions should be bold, frequent, energetic, using bright colors and dynamic entrance animations.
Group shots, candid action, and high-energy moments are prioritized over quiet portraits.
Pacing should feel rhythmic and exciting, like a highlight reel.
